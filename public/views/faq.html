Frequenty Asked Questions

  • What is Swappable?

    Swappable is a digital gift card app that makes gift giving easy. With Swappable, users can easily send eGift via email or text with personalized text message, video and photo collage. Recipients can then claim the gift card or swap it for another gift card of choice.

  • What is the Swappable Wallet?

    The Swappable Wallet enables you to manage your reward dollars and purchased or received gift cards.

  • What is the difference between claimed and unclaimed gift card?

    Recipients of Swappable gift cards have two options. They can either claim or swap their gift card.

    When a user claims a gift card, the gift card is added to their Swappable Wallet.

    If a user chooses to swap their gift card, they can select another gift card of equal value from the gift card catalog. Once the swapped card has been claimed, it is added to the Swappable Wallet.

  • What countries does Swappable currently work in?

    Currently, Swappable is only available in the United States.

  • Do I earn points or rewards when I purchase and send a gift card?

    Yes! You earn cash back on most gift card purchases that can be used on a future purchase.

  • How do I use my cash rewards?

    Your cash rewards are stored in your Swappable Wallet. When you purchase a gift card, you can choose to apply some or all of your cash rewards to your purchase at checkout.

  • Can I purchase gift card for myself?

    Absolutely! You can purchase gift cards for yourself and still earn cash rewards for future purchases.

  • Do I need to purchase gift card to send a personalized message?

    You do not need to purchase a gift card to take advantage of the personalized swappable messages. You can send a message with text, video, and photos by selecting the Send Message button on the home screen. These messages can be sent to others via text message or email.

  • Can my gift cards be used in store or online?

    Of course! Simply show the cashier the barcode to scan during checkout. You can also use the gift card number in a lieu of the bar code. Use gift card number during checkout for online transactions.

  • What can I use my gift card for?

    You can use any claimed gift cards in you Swappable Wallet at any of our retailers. Gift cards can be used towards both in-store and online purchases.

  • Does my gift card expire?

    Gift cards do not expire. Per the CARD Act of 2009, an inactivity fee is applied if gift card has not been used 12 months after the purchase date.
